There is only one < a > at the end of < taca > in the original. As Cordova gives this a first person form, we assume there must be a missing final < a >, or -- more commonly post < a > -- a missing < ya >. This is a bit surprising. Perhaps the form given here is a slightly artificial form for expository purposes.
